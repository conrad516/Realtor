Real Estate Services

A sold home is not the end but the beginning of a life long relationship. Conrad is always looking to help his past and present clients improve their lending needs. He takes pride in ensuring the people he works with are the most educated buyers and/or sellers on the market. Conrad gathers a genuine understanding of his customers and clients needs, build positive rapport, customize a plan to meet their specific goals and time frame, then he utilizes his impeccable negotiation skills to get his clients the best possible price under the best terms and conditions. Conrad has been told by people in the industry that he is a knowledgeable, trustworthy, dedicated, and thoroughly trained REALTOR. He comes highly recommended and would be happy to provide you with references upon request. Conrad has always maintained his ethical integrity and has developed lifelong referals and incredible relationships with the people he does business with. Conrad works with a team of extremely dedicated and highly productive Real Estate professionals that speak over thirty languages and service the United States.
